Mars and Beyond August 18-19 2012 from 10am to 4pm
at the Museum of Science Boston

Date: August 18-19 2012
Times: 10am to 4pm
Location: Museum of Science
1 Science Park, Boston, MA 02114

Join us for "Mars and Beyond," a weekend event at the Museum of Science, Boston, on August 18-19 2012 from 10:00 am to 4:00 pm. Adults and kids alike will enjoy displays and demonstrations about Mars, the Moon, the Sun, and many other bodies within and outside our Solar System. Try a Mars Rover simulator game and get the latest updates about the Curiosity rover expected to land on Mars on August 6. Immerse yourself in a special exhibit about the International Space Station. Meet NASA-funded scientists and engineers, talk with a NASA astronaut, and participate in panel discussions and hands-on activities.
